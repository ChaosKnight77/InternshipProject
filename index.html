How Imagiobox Subscription Works

Imagiobox and ColorPencil combo provides your kid a smart way to develop creativity. You can subscribe to IMAGIO box based on any of our monthly subscription plans.

After subscribing you will receive a customized art activity box designed for your child's age group at your doorstep every month.

You will get regular updates from us on the registered mobile number and email id about the delivery of the box.

Imagiobox subscribers get access to the smart app ColorPencil which is specially created for kids to track their progress and participate in several events and competitions organised frequently.
